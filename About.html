About

Multimedia Artist

Lam Le is a Melbourne-based multimedia designer who graduated in 2024 with a Master of Animation, Games, and Interactivity from RMIT University. Specializing in 3D animation, modeling, and environment design. Her work is known for its vibrant, colorful approach, spanning across graphic design, illustration, web design, videography, and animation. Lam's recent project, 'Coffin Room,' has been selected for several animation festivals, including FIVARS, QLD XR, and Open World Animation, showcasing her ability to create engaging and immersive visual experiences.
