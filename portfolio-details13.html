Blob
This is a part of my Master studio project. I created an interactive game in Unity with the theme of transformation with the purpose of experimenting with character animation and environment.


Gameplay

In this RPG simulation game, players control a morphing black blob that explores a mysterious world. As the blob moves through different environments, it gains four new abilities, each based on a sense: vision, hearing, mouth, and hand. These abilities allow the blob to interact with its surroundings in unique ways—vision inverts the colors of the environment, hearing brings background music to life, mouth enables conversations with NPCs, and hand provides the power to shoot enemies. The journey culminates in a final battle with a boss. Upon victory, the blob consumes the boss, transforming into a human and opening a portal, allowing the game to restart with new possibilities.
