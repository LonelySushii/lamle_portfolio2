Valsia
This project is a 3D environment design initiative that I developed during my bachelor's year at RMIT University. It highlights the environmental design of a fantastical, abandoned world that I created to explore concept art and world-building. I designed the assets using Blender and then assembled them, conducting the walking test in Unreal Engine 5.



Story Lore

Valsia is a land where god and human used to live. However, due to an unknown reason, everyone suddenly disappears leaving the place abandoned. Over the time, the buildings and monuments deteriorates and are covered by bushes and flowers.







Concept Art

Perched atop the highest mountains in the world, the city rises above the clouds. From a distance, it resembles a colossal ancient tower, showcasing strong Greek architectural influences. There are three pathways leading to the city, created from numerous high cliffs that form a bridge to Valsia. Off in the distance, a mysterious tower stands, appearing as though half of it has been shattered by an immense force.
