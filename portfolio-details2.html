CPS Candy Jar

  • Category: Product Design
  • Client: University Work
  • Project date: October, 2023
  • Description : This is my archival object for one of my master course. It's a candy jar filled with a variety of candies, each with unique packaging designs inspired by the creative projects I completed during weeks 1 to 9 of my course. The idea for this project came to me while enjoying my favorite candies. Most of my work typically revolves around digital creations, so the idea of transforming abstract concepts into tangible, edible forms that I personally enjoy intrigued me. Imagine being able to consume and share digital ideas in a physical, edible format! I also created a physical prototype of the product.
